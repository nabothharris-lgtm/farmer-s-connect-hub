# AgriConnect 🌱
**Uganda's Agricultural Marketplace**

Connecting Ugandan farmers with verified agro experts, input stores, and field agents. Book services, order inputs, track your farm, and grow your harvest with the right people beside you.

## 🚀 Overview

AgriConnect is a comprehensive platform built to bridge the gap in the agricultural sector. It provides a single marketplace where every side wins when farmers grow more.

### Four Key Roles

- **🧑‍🌾 Farmers**: Find nearby experts, order inputs safely, and track your farm history.
- **👩‍🔬 Experts**: Get booked by farmers near you and build your reputation.
- **🏪 Agro Stores**: List your agricultural inputs, fulfill orders, and grow your visibility.
- **🗺️ Field Agents**: Onboard farmers and earn commission on every transaction.

## ✨ Features

- **📍 GPS Matching**: Find the closest experts and stores based on your farm's location.
- **⭐ Verified Experts**: Work with rated and vetted agricultural professionals.
- **💳 Escrow-Ready Payments**: Pay safely. Funds are only released when the work is done or items are delivered.
- **🤖 AI Support**: Features an integrated Landbot assistant for quick answers and guidance.

## 🛠️ How it Works

1. **Sign up & Locate**: Create your free account and share your farm's location.
2. **Book a Service / Order Inputs**: Pick a verified expert, schedule a visit, or buy the supplies you need.
3. **Pay & Rate**: Pay through the platform safely, and rate your experience after the service is completed.

## 💻 Tech Stack
- **Frontend**: React, TanStack Start, TailwindCSS, Radix UI
- **Backend / DB**: Supabase
- **Deployment**: Vite, Cloudflare
